⚠ NZ Council Shake-up: Regional councils confirmed for abolition before 2028 elections

On 5 May 2026, the NZ government gave all 78 councils a 3-month deadline (by 9 Aug 2026) to submit amalgamation proposals or have reform imposed. Regional councillors will not be elected in 2028. Territorial authority mergers are expected to follow. If Labour wins the Nov 2026 election, this entire reform could be reversed.
